Macarons

Ingredients

  • 200g almond flour
  • 200g powdered sugar
  • 110g egg whites (about 3-4 eggs)
  • 1/4 tsp cream of tartar
  • 100g granulated sugar
  • food coloring (optional)

Steps

  1. In a food processor, grind the almond flour and powdered sugar together until fine.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites and cream of tartar together until they form stiff peaks.
  3. Gradually add in the granulated sugar and continue to beat until the mixture becomes shiny and glossy.
  4. If desired, add food coloring at this point and mix well.
  5. Carefully fold in the almond flour and powdered sugar mixture into the egg white mixture.
  6. Transfer the mixture into a pastry bag fitted with a round tip.
  7. Pipe the mixture onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving about 1-2 inches between each macaron.
  8. Allow the macarons to sit out for about 30 minutes to an hour to form a "skin" on the surface.
  9. Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C) and bake the macarons for 12-15 minutes.
  10. Allow the macarons to cool completely before filling with your choice of filling (such as buttercream or ganache) and sandwiching them together.
