Croissant

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Milk or water
  • Butter (for traditional croissants) or margarine (for non-dairy croissants)

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, yeast, salt, and sugar. Mix well.
  2. Add milk or water and mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rise for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  5. Roll out the dough into a large rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.
  6. Spread chilled butter or margarine over 2/3 of the dough, leaving a 1/2 inch border around the edges.
  7. Fold the unbuttered third of the dough over the middle third, then fold the remaining buttered third over that.
  8. Roll out the dough again into a large rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick.
  9. Fold the dough into thirds again and roll out one more time.
  10. Cut the dough into triangles and roll up each triangle from the wide end to the point.
  11. Place the croissants on a baking sheet and let them rise for another 30 minutes.
  12. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and brush the croissants with beaten egg.
  13. Bake for about 15-20 minutes, or until golden brown.
